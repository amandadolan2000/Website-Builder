FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need any technical knowledge?

Not at all. I handle everything and explain it in plain English. You just need to tell me about your business.

How does hosting work if you give me files?

I'll point you to free options like Netlify where you drag-and-drop a folder and you're live in minutes. Or add the $75 setup add-on and I'll handle it for you.

What if I want changes after delivery?

Revision rounds are included in every package. After that, small edits are available at a flat rate so you're never stuck.

How long does it take?

Usually 3–5 business days from when I have all your info. Premium clients get priority turnaround.

Do you do the free mockup first?

Yes — reach out and I'll put together a basic version of your site before you commit to anything. No pressure at all.

Can you help me get a domain name?

Absolutely. I can walk you through buying a domain and connecting it to your site. It's simpler than it sounds.
